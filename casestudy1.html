WellRead

WellRead provides a curated list of top-rated books and allows users to find books in their local communities for book swaps and book clubs.

Jennifer

Jennifer needs to have easy and quick access to trustworthy literature recommendations so that she can spend less time browsing and more time expanding her mind and reading community.

Problem

Users want to read more but do not know where to begin finding their particular taste in books as well as local book-reading communities

Solution

WellRead provides a curated list of top-rated books and allows users to find books in their local communities, book swaps, book clubs, and independent book stores.

Tools Used

Trello, Miro, Figma, FigJam, Google G-Suite

My Role

I played multiple roles at times on the project. At times I was a product manager. I organized our deliverables to ensure we met deadlines. I did a range of research including a survey, social science research on the population, a user research plan, and conducted a usability test. I did sketches and designed Figma wireframes for onboarding and individual book details frames. I also contributed to the mid/high fidelity prototype and branding for the app. at other times I was a researcher and designer. I helped us meet deadlines throughout the project. I organized our deliverables and timelines.
